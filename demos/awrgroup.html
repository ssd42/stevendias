Building on 75 Years of Excellence

Founded in 1947, AWR Group has a performance record reflecting the leadership and commitment to long-term relationships of the principals at its heart. Every principal is active in the day-to-day running of the company, meeting with potential and established clients.

"Precisely and efficiently, with advanced technological know-how, AWR specializes in structural repair, roofing, historic restoration, and exterior cleaning on private and commercial buildings of all types."

Our flexible interaction inspires a dedicated staff able to communicate and negotiate among the varied requirements of owners, tenants, architects, and engineers, demonstrating the teamwork necessary to ensure that quality control can be exactingly maintained.

AWR's project managers are skilled, caring professionals who are introduced at the beginning of each project and remain in charge until its completion. Their attention to detail and pride in craftsmanship provide for effective and thorough project management.
