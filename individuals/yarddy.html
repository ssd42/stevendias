About Coach Yarddy

With over three decades of coaching experience, Yarddy Jaramillo has dedicated his career to developing young soccer talent in Newark, NJ and beyond.

A former coach of the Ecuadorian U-16 national team, Yarddy brings international expertise to local training, helping players of all ages reach their full potential.

As a pillar of the Newark community, Yarddy is committed to not just coaching soccer skills, but building character, discipline, and teamwork in every player he trains.
